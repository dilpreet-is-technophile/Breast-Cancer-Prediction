Breast cancer is a type of cancer that starts in the breast. Cancer starts when cells begin to grow out of control.
There are two main classifications of tumors. One is known as benign and the other as malignant.

What this predicts?

This model predicts that by what percent is there possibility of a breast tumor to be "benign" or "malignant".

  • Benign: This type of tumor is not dangerous and donot convert into cancer tumor
  • Malignant: This type of tumor is dangerous and it can spread and become cancer

  • 'radius_se'
  • 'concavity_mean'
  • 'compactness_worst'
  • 'concave points_worst'

How it predicts?

  • Features like 'area-mean' of tumor and other features are taken
  • On the basis of these features classified as 'benign' or 'malignant'
  • The features can be extracted from a X-Ray image
  • X-Ray image is not used,text data of X-ray image is used
This Model uses features of a tumor such as 'area-mean' of the tumor which are attained using X-ray images of tumors from breast and classifies them as 'benign' or 'malignant' on the basis of these features
